Current projects

MusicSync

MusicSync is a simple music syning service that does just one thing and tries to do it well.

Music Sync currently consists of three components

  • Backend - A central daemon that controls everything.
  • Web - A frontend for the backend daemon for controlling it and streaming music from it.
  • Android - A Android app frontent for the backend daemon for syning music to your phone.
